Frequently Asked Questions

1. Yumway ke products kaha se kharidein?

Aap hamare contact page ya dealer network ke zariye directly order kar sakte hain.

2. Delivery kitne dino me hoti hai?

Generally 3-5 working days ke andar aapko product mil jaata hai.

Haan, Yumway ke sabhi products frozen hain aur aapko cold-chain ke through milte hain.

4. Payment kaise kar sakte hain?

Aap UPI, Net Banking, ya COD (Cash on Delivery) options ka use kar sakte hain.
